How to download Vmware WorkStation Pro

Official Download Page

Go to the official VMware download pageofficial website

Once there, you’ll land on the login/registration screen. Click REGISTER in the top-right corner to sign up.

Registration Steps

On the registration page, fill in your name, email, password, etc. Be sure to check your email for the verification code. Once you’re verified, head back to the login page.


Just a heads-up: the ‘Username’ field is for your email, not your name.

⚠️ Trouble Logging In?

If you can’t log in, try switching to a different network node (VPN). If it still doesn’t work, follow these alternative steps:

  1. Go to the VMware Workstation Pro official download page. official site

  2. In the top-right corner, click Broadcom Support.

  3. You’re about to be redirected to another site — just follow the steps below, one by one.

  4. When the login page pops up, enter your email and password.

Download Steps

  1. After logging in, go to My Downloads.

  2. Click the HERE button to search.


3. Type in “VMware Workstation Pro”, then click on the result.

Final Steps

Pick your OS —— Windows or Linux

Pick the version you want.

Click to check out the Terms and Conditions, then exit the page before you can tick ‘I agree to the’.

Fill in the required info (you can just use placeholder data)

Then, hit the Download button again and you’re good to go!
